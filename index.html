Welcome on our website !

Welcome to our website! "Economists and jurists" is a project of the prosopographical study of economists and jurists from the XIXth century to now.

This project will be carried out using the data made available on the DBpedia, Wikidata and BnF Data SPARQL endpoints. The data will be aggregated and cleansed, the final aim is to obtain a certain volume of consistent information, expressed with a coherent ontology, in order to implement some analysis and identify some characteristic and structural aspects of this population.

Inside this website, you will find different types of information.
First, it is possible to find explanations on the whole process which allowed us to have a clean and accessible endpoint. In addition, all the statistical analysis that we have carried out are available and are explained.
Finally, a detailed documentation is available to understand more about our approach. As well as access to tools that could facilitate our work.
